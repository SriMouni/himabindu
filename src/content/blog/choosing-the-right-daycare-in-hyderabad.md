For working parents, choosing the right **daycare in Hyderabad** means finding a place that is safe, nurturing, and stimulating. A great daycare is more than supervision — it's a second home where your child feels secure and continues to learn.

## What to prioritise

- **Safety & security:** Secure premises, controlled access, CCTV, and clear pick-up protocols.
- **Hygiene:** Clean play areas, sanitised toys, and healthy washroom and meal routines.
- **Trained caregivers:** Warm, qualified staff with low child-to-caregiver ratios.
- **Nutrition:** Balanced, age-appropriate meals and snacks.
- **Routine:** Predictable rhythms of play, learning, meals, and rest.

## Questions every parent should ask

1. What are the safety and emergency procedures?
2. How are meals and naps managed?
3. What is the caregiver-to-child ratio?
4. How do you communicate my child's day to me?
5. Is there a learning component, or only supervision?
