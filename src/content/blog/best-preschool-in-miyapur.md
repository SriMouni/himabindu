## What makes a great preschool in Miyapur

- **Modern, safe, hygienic** facilities designed for young children.
- **Play-based, activity-led learning** rather than rote work.
- **Trained, caring educators** with individual attention.
- **Daycare option** for working parents, with meals and rest.
- **Transparent communication** so you always know how your child is doing.
