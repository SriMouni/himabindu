Thinking about enrolling your child? Here's a simple, step-by-step guide to the **FirstCry Intellitots admission process** across the Hyderabad centres [managed by Himabindu Rudrapaka](/himabindu-rudrapaka).

## Step 1: Make an enquiry

Start by sending an admission enquiry for your preferred centre — [Nizampet](/schools/firstcry-intellitots-nizampet), [Botanical Garden](/schools/firstcry-intellitots-botanical-garden), [HMT Hills](/schools/firstcry-intellitots-hmt-hills), or [Mayuri Nagar](/schools/firstcry-intellitots-mayuri-nagar). Each school page has an **admission enquiry** button.

## Step 2: Visit the centre

Schedule a visit to see the classrooms, meet the educators, and get a feel for the environment. This is the best moment to ask your questions and watch how children and teachers interact.

## Step 3: Choose the right program

Programs are offered by age and need:

- **Playgroup** — toddlers beginning their early-learning journey.
- **Nursery** — building language, motor, and social skills.
- **Junior & Senior KG** — school-readiness and foundational learning.
- **Daycare** — full-day care with meals, naps, and activities.

## Step 4: Complete enrolment

Once you've chosen a program, the team will guide you through enrolment, required documents, and the start date.

## Questions worth asking

- What are the timings and daycare hours?
- What's the child-to-teacher ratio?
- How is safety, hygiene, and pick-up handled?
- How will I get updates on my child's progress?
- What is the current fee structure for my chosen program?

For fees and exact timings — which vary by centre and program — it's best to **ask the centre directly** during your enquiry or visit. Ready to begin? [Explore the schools](/schools-managed-by-himabindu-rudrapaka) and send an enquiry.
